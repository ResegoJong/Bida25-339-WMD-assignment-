Overview of Campuses

BAC has two main campuses: the Main Campus and the New Campus. Each has different facilities and access points.

Main Campus Directions

  • Main gate is for cars only (students use small gate).
  • Walk straight to reach the main junction.

Key Locations Inside Main Campus

  • Left at junction: BAC Clinic and hostels
  • Right side: Entrance to academic blocks

New Campus Directions
