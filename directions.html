BAC Campus Directions Guide

Overview of Campuses

BAC has two main campuses: the Main Campus and the New Campus. Each has different facilities and access points.

Main Campus Directions

  • Main gate is for cars only (students use small gate after first day).
  • After entering small gate, you will see a parking lot.
  • Walk straight to reach the main junction.

Key Locations Inside Main Campus

  • Left at junction: BAC Clinic and hostels
  • Straight ahead: labelled caravans and lab areas
  • Right side: entrance to academic blocks

Inside Academic Area

  • Lab 3 is immediately on your right when entering
  • Left side: printing room, room 021, male toilets, room 022, lab, female toilets
  • Right side: additional labs, lecture offices, student inquiry desk
  • Stairs left: lecture hall
  • Stairs right: more labs and lecture rooms

New Campus Directions

  • Student entry gate is used for walking access
  • Car entry gate is separate
  • After entry, you will see a parking lot and two main blocks

Blocks at New Campus

  • Block A: Lecturer and tutor offices
  • Block B: Attendance block

Block B floors:

  • B001 - Ground floor
  • B100 - 1st floor
  • B200 - 2nd floor
  • B300 - 3rd floor
  • B400 - 4th floor
  • Library is on the left side between the two blocks
  • Block B entrances on right between the two blocks

Campus Facilities Note

  • Clinic: 07:00 - 00:00
  • Library: 07:00 - 19:00
  • All Nighter: 17:00 - 23:59 (leave allowed), 00:00 - 06:00 (no leaving)
  • Gradebook: 24/7 access
